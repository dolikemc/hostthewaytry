Dear host, ... let's start!

We welcome you to www.hosttheway.com! we are a non-profit organization that was created for the benfit of inviduals not large cooperation. That means you get 100% of the money you charge your guests - we take no fees or commission. The purpose of our company is to connect travellers with the authentic locals. We made the company wuth two things in mind: to benefit the traveller with a true cultural experience and to put money in the hands of people who need it most.

Our principal advertising campaign in the first stages of our company will be the word of mouth. Regarding that, it is important that each guest you host receives an authentic cultural and culinary experience.

The volunteer that is signing up your home does this with the true sprit of travelling at heart and your future benefit in mind. Therefore we kindly ask you to give them a place to spend the night as well as a warm meal to honor their effort. This will also give you the time to answer the questionnaire and help us to get to know you and your home.

How does it work?

  • We will put up your listing on our homepage including a detailed description and photos of your house
  • Travellers will contact you via email
  • You will be paid in cash by the travellers upon their arrival
  • If you no longer want to participate, please inform us via email and we will take your listing off the page
  • We take no liability for the travellers or the hosts
